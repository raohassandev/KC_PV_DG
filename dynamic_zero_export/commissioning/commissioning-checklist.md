# Dynamic Zero Export Commissioning Checklist

1. Confirm upstream meter type and transport.
2. Confirm inverter brand/profile.
3. Confirm topology and bus count.
4. Confirm generator count, type, and ratings.
5. Confirm tie/breaker signals.
6. Confirm virtual meter emulation profile.
7. Confirm policy mode and fallback mode.
8. Confirm LAN/WiFi monitoring access.
9. Verify pass-through mode.
10. Verify adjusted mode.
11. Verify zero-export behavior.
12. Verify generator minimum-load behavior.
13. Verify stale-data fallback.
14. Verify alarms and event log.
